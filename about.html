About us

WE ARE BUILDING CRITICAL INFRASTRUCTURE TO STRENGTHEN AFRICA'S HEALTH SYSTEMS â€“ FROM OUTPATIENT CLINICS AND TEACHING HOSPITALS, TO GOVERNMENTS AND DONOR AGENCIES

Since 2016, Techno HMS has taken hospitals, clinics and other health facilities digital almost instantly using our flagship Electronic Medical Records/Hospital Management Information System product. Now, our intuitive and versatile suite of technologies is enabling Providers utilize telemedicine and receive additional financing, Payers validate beneficiaries and process claims quicker, Patients take control of their care, and other Partners receive unprecedented epidemiological insights and real-world evidence, all resulting in significantly better quality of care.

Today, Techno HMS is the #1 healthcare technology provider in West Africa, facilitating hundreds of thousands of encounters each month across the region. And we are just getting started. We are inspired by a vision of a connected healthcare ecosystem across Africa that simplifies health access, making it easy for patients to get the care they need when they need it. We are dedicated to empowering all healthcare stakeholders with the actionable data and tools with which they can make informed decisions and transform global health. We are on a mission to make world-class healthcare a reality for the 6 billion people in emerging markets, and we're determined to deliver this future.
